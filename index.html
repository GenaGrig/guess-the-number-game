How to play:

In this game you need to guess the random number, chosen by AI in different challenges. You will get hints about guessed number range and how many attempts are left. Good luck!

    Difficulty levels:
  • Easy: 1 to 10 in 3 attempts
  • Medium: 1 to 100 in 10 attempts
  • Hard: 1 to 500 in 9 attempts
  • Hardest 1 to 1000 in 8 attempts
