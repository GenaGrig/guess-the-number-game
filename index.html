How to play:

In this game you need to guess the random number, chosen by AI in different levels of difficulty. After every guess you will get a hint, if your number was less or higher than chosen by AI and how many attempts are left. Good luck!

    Difficulty levels:

  • Easy: 1 to 10 in 3 attempts
  • Medium: 1 to 100 in 10 attempts
  • Hard: 1 to 500 in 9 attempts
  • Hardest: 1 to 1000 in 8 attempts
