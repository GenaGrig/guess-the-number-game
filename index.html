Guess the number!

Rules:

In this game you need to guess the random number, chosen by AI in different challenges.

  • 1 to 10 in 3 attempts
  • 1 to 100 in 10 attempts
  • 1 to 1000 in 15 attempts
After every guess you will get a hint, if you number was less or higher than guessed by AI.
